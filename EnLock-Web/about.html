About ENlock IT Solutions!

Our Story

Welcome to ENlock ITS, your number one source for all things IT. We're dedicated to giving you the very best of our IT services, with a focus on communication, adaptability, future proofing. Founded in 2020 by Christopher Cook, and Isaac Lyne, ENlock IT Solutions has come a long way from its beginnings in the university UCOL located in Palmerston North. When Chris and Isaac first started out, their passion for IT drove them to study harder, and put all available time and money into ENlock, this is so that ENlock ITS can offer you the highest quality and proffesionalism during your project. We now serve customers all over Palmerston North, and are thrilled that we're able to turn our passion into our own website. We hope you enjoy our products and services as much as we enjoy offering & producing them for you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
